DIY PS

These are the following tools to create a drawing.

press 1 for a gray circle for a cats head

press 2 for small black circles for the nose and mouth

press 3 for two different colored triangles for the ears

press 4 for a grey line to shape the cat

press 5 for a brown small line for the line

press 6 for two small white circles for the eyes

press 7 for black small lines for the whiskers

press 8 for a grey small oval for the smaller paws

press 9 for a darker grey oval for the bigger paws

press 0 for multicolor circles as lights or as other heads for different cats

press p for a screenshot of your work
